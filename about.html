About Sampada Trust

Sampada Trust began as an informal outreach program of Watershed Organisation Trust (WOTR) in 1998 to assist women and SHGs in the area of skills and capacity enhancement, for access to and acquisition of assets and resources.

Sampada Trust aims at empowering rural women by providing financial, entrepreneurial and customized services to women through their SHGs and village level Apex Bodies - Sanyukta Mahila Samittee (SMS) to take up quality of life enhancing measures and income-generating activities.
