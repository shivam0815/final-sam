Sampada Trust - Steps Towards Empowerment

I. Social Capital Formation
Promotion of Women's Self-Help Groups

The main focus of the Trust is SELF-HELP - A Key to Women's Empowerment and Self-Development. Efficient planning, budgeting and savings are essential components for economic sustainability.

Sampada believes in organizing women into SHGs, regularly practicing saving with internal lending. These SHGs are federated at the village level, called the Sanyukta Mahila Samitee (SMS).

Capacity Building

Empowerment is a process of capacity building of people. Sampada Trust regularly organizes training, exchanges, and exposures on relevant themes for SHGs, SMSs, Women Promoters, NGOs, and staff.

II. Micro Credit: Capitalization

Micro finance has become a major tool for development. Sampada Trust was instrumental in providing timely credit to SHGs through SMS until 31 March 2013.

III. Micro-Enterprise

Sampada Trust provides motivational, information, and managerial inputs to SHGs through SMSs for undertaking income-generating activities and micro-enterprises.

IV. Micro-Insurance: Social Security

This is a new intervention of Sampada. Women in the villages felt the need to secure their lives and livelihoods; however, the insurance service was not accessible to the rural poor.

V. Social Development Activities

The representative body of women's groups - the SMS, consolidates the concerns of women and finds solutions to the expressed socio-economic demands.

They implement activities such as drinking water, sanitation, health, and hygiene promotions, which have made Sampada's women self-confident contributors to the development process.

Aims & Approach

The aim of Sampada Trust is to empower women by providing opportunities to secure sustainable livelihood and position them in the local decision-making process.

To achieve this objective, Sampada seeks to mobilize the creative potential of groups and individuals by empowering them and providing financial, entrepreneurial, and customized services to enable them to come together to secure and enhance their quality of life.

Sampada Trust also seeks to assist NGOs and CBOs interested in women's empowerment, extending them support to build up their capacities, as well as by networking and linkage building.
